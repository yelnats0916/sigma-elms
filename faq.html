Login & Access

What is my Login ID?

Your Login ID is the unique identification number found on your official School ID card. If you are a new student and don't have an ID yet, please refer to your Registration Form.

What is the default password?

By default, your password is your Birthday in YYYYMMDD format (e.g., if you were born on Jan 15, 2007, your password is 20070115). We highly recommend changing it after your first login.

How do I reset a forgotten password?

If you can still access your school-provided email, click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to you. If not, please visit the IT Support Office for a manual reset.

Why is my account inactive?

Accounts may be set to inactive if there are pending enrollment issues or if the account hasn't been used for two consecutive semesters. Contact the Registrar for reactivation.

Can I use the portal on multiple devices?

Yes, the portal is responsive and works on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. For security, we recommend not staying logged in on public computers.

Student Guides

How do I view my grades?

Once logged in, navigate to the Gradebook tab. You can select the specific grading period (Prelims, Midterms, Finals) to see your detailed score breakdown.

How to submit an assignment?

Go to your Active Courses, click on the assignment link, and use the Add Submission button to upload your file. Supported formats are usually PDF, DOCX, and ZIP.

Where can I see my class schedule?

Your weekly class schedule is available on the Dashboard under the "Weekly Calendar" section. You can also download a PDF version from the Enrollment tab.

How do I join an online lecture?

When it's time for your class, a Join Session button will appear in the specific course module. This will redirect you to the virtual meeting room (e.g., Zoom or Google Meet).

Is there a mobile app available?

While there isn't a dedicated app store download yet, the website is optimized for mobile browsers. You can "Add to Home Screen" on your phone for quick access.

Teacher Support

How to encode student grades?

Access the Faculty Portal, select your class section, and click on Encode Grades. Ensure you click 'Save & Sync' to update the student records in real-time.

How do I create a new quiz?

Under the Course Management menu, select Quiz Builder. You can choose from Multiple Choice, True/False, or Essay formats and set the timer for each quiz.

How to upload syllabus materials?

Within your course page, navigate to Resources and click the Upload File button. You can organize folders by "Prelim", "Midterm", and "Final" content.

Where can I see student feedback?

End-of-semester evaluations and student feedback are compiled under the Performance Reviews tab in the Faculty Dashboard once the grading period ends.

How do I generate a class list?

Go to Class Records, select your section, and click the Export Attendance/Class List icon. You can download it as an Excel or PDF file.
